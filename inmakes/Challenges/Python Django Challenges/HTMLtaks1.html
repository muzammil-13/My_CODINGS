iPHONE 15 Launched!

12 September 2023 

Apple announced the iPhone 15 series at its Wonderlust event on September 12.
Pre-orders for the iPhone 15 series began on September 15 and they will be available
in stores on September 22.
The iPhone 15 series includes:
~ A baseline iPhone 15
~ A larger iPhone 15 Plus
~ A pair of Pro models, the iPhone 15 Pro and iPhone 15 Pro Max
~ Two 6.1-inch iPhones and two 6.7-inch iPhones

The iPhone 15 models have a new dynamic island instead of the older notch design. They are available in five colors: pink, yellow, green, blue, and black. The iPhone 15 Pro and iPhone 15 Pro Max are available in four finishes: black titanium, white titanium, blue titanium, and natural titanium.
